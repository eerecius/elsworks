SILICA

SILICA is a browser-based incremental game about picking up sand in a desert. It is in very early development (0.1.1-alpha, to be exact) but has a large body of planned content (goats, millipedes, wheels, etc) that will be implemented once I find the time to devote to it. In the meanwhile, feel free to poke around and let me know what you think!

You can launch the game by clicking above, or by navigating to silica.els.works in your web browser. It's up to you.
