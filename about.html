Hi all --

My name is Ellory and I'm a mixed-media artist currently living & working in Santa Cruz, CA. I make art in a variety of media and covering a variety of subjects; as of late I've been fascinated by cardboard, ink, and oil paint. I've always loved narrative and as such, much of my work involves building up a world and a story beyond the picture plane through little hints and morsels embedded throughout.

Even for an artist my focus can be very mercureal, but recently I've been exploring intentional deviations from linear perspective, faults and breakdowns in communication, the unexpressed and the left-unsaid.
