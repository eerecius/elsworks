about
My name is Ellory and I'm a painter and comic artist currently working in Santa Cruz, CA. I make art in a variety of media and covering a variety of subjects; as of late I've been fascinated by cardboard, ink, and oil paint. Much of my work involves building up a world beyond the picture plane through hints and morsels embedded throughout. Even for an artist my focus can be very mercureal, but recently I've been exploring deviations from linear perspective, breakdowns in communication, and the unexpressed or left-unsaid.

I am a big fan of comics for their versatility and expressiveness as a storytelling medium -- though I have only been making comics for a few years, I have covered genres ranging from historical fiction to magical realism to autobiography.

If you're interested in purchasing any of my pieces or would like to collaborate on a project, don't hesitate to reach out!
