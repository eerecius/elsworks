blue is my 1978 centurion lemans . . . she was liberated from a man on craigslist for $125 and since has been pampered with regular cleans and new components. she is originally from the bike boom, so she came with stem shifters and those weird extra brake levers which I am in the process of removing. this specific model of lemans is made with tange hi-tensile steel and although she's built like a truck she still rides nice and smooth.

currently im putting the finishing touches on blue and starting to research my next project: my first fixed gear! i rode single speeds all through high school and love not worrying about if my gearing is "optimal" or not. although converting an old 10speed would be the cheapest option, i love 10speeds too much to "destroy" one like that so i'm after a 62cm track frame to start out!
